Our Story

Built on Faith,
Driven by Purpose

Founded on September 8, 1995 by Mr. Reylindo Ortega and his wife Ms. Myrna T. Ortega, First Corinthians began as a small enterprise with a big heart — to provide dignified livelihood to workers by matching their skills to real business needs.

On December 23, 2002, the organization was formally converted into a cooperative under the Cooperative Development Authority, becoming the First Corinthians Multi-Purpose Cooperative (FCMC) — a name that reflects its founding values of faith, service, and community.

"We are, where God wants us to be."

Our Mission

  • Provide livelihood by matching members' skills to client business needs
  • Help members achieve financial stability and security

Our Vision

  • To be a HOME to members

The FCMC Advantage

Why Choose FCMC?

We're not just a service provider — we're a compliant, tax-exempt cooperative that saves you money and protects your business.

01

Tax-Exempt Cooperative

Unlike regular manpower agencies, FCMC is a BIR-registered tax-exempt cooperative — meaning no 12% VAT charged to you.

02

DOLE-DO174 Accredited

Fully compliant and accredited by the Department of Labor and Employment. Your business is protected and legally covered.

03

Background Checked

Every worker undergoes thorough background investigation before deployment — ensuring you always get verified, trustworthy personnel.

04

Seamless Payroll

Dedicated account officers process daily time records and ensure all mandatory government benefits are filed on time.

05

Effortless Recruitment

Our HR team handles all sourcing and qualifying. We endorse only candidates who pass our rigorous screening process.

06

Violation Management

FCMC issues proper disciplinary actions for offenders and handles DOLE cases — keeping your workplace disciplined and compliant.

How It Works

Simple Process,
Fast Deployment

From contract signing to worker deployment in as fast as 10 working days.

01

Sign Contract

Client signs a service agreement with FCMC. Even a single personnel request is accommodated.

02

Sourcing & Screening

Our HR team sources candidates, conducts background checks, and runs qualifying interviews within 10 working days.

03

Deployment Notice

Client receives a formal notice of deployment with the worker's details after all pre-deployment requirements are submitted.

04

30-Day Evaluation

Client evaluates the worker's performance for up to one month. If unsatisfactory, FCMC provides a replacement at no extra burden.

05

Ongoing Support

FCMC continues managing payroll, benefits, DTR, and compliance — you simply focus on your core business.
